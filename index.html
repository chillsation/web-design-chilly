About me

Hey, I'm Gia! I like to sketch and make illustrations. I have skills with both traditional and digital but I prefer working with the latter. I'm a self-taught artist based in the Philippines, and I'm currently a third-year college student.

I love making both fanart and my own character designs, and I also like to experiment with different art styles. I often keep myself busy by learning different skills as a way to keep myself open-minded to different thinking and influences. I'm inspired by animation and visual storytelling and I hope to one day make my own work where I can be proud to show for everyone to see.
